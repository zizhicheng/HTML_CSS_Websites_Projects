Pseudo-classes ---

  1. link --- a:link {color: red}
  2. visited --- a:visited {color: red} --- visited link will show red
  3. hover --- h1:hover {color: red}
    --- when the mouse moves over any h1 the text temporarily changes to red
  4. active --- a:active {color:red} --- link is currently being visited will show red
  5. empty --- p:empty {color: red} --- empty element will show red
